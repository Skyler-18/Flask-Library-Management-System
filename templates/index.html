Apni Library

Apni Library is an e-library platform where students can request e-books. Once approved by librarian, students gain access to the book for 7 days. After this period, access is automatically revoked. Librarian have the authority to revoke access before the 7-day limit, and students can also return books early. Each student can request a maximum of 5 books at a time.
